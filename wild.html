LION

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large cat of the genus Panthera, currently ranging only in Sub-Saharan Africa and India.

TIGER

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is a large cat and a member of the genus Panthera native to Asia.

PEACOCK

The Indian peacock (Pavo cristatus) has iridescent blue and green plumage, mostly metal-like blue and green.
